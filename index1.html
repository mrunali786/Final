About State Bank of India (SBI)

State Bank of India (SBI) is the largest public sector bank in India and a leading financial institution in the country. It was founded on July 1, 1955, and has since been serving millions of customers with its diverse range of banking and financial services.

To be the most preferred and trusted bank, enhancing the quality of life for all our stakeholders, through responsive and innovative financial solutions.

Our Mission

To provide a wide range of customer-centric financial products and services, delivered through an extensive network of branches, ATMs, and digital channels, ensuring excellent customer experience.
